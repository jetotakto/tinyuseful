How it works

Every number tool uses the same formula your bank uses, computed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Word tools use a built-in dictionary and standard syllable rules — no external lookup, no API call. Open the page once, and it works offline forever.

Why no AI

AI is great at writing emails. It is a wasteful, unverifiable tool for arithmetic and grammar. These ten tools have one correct answer each. No probability, no hallucination, no "let me try that again." For when you don't need a chat — just an answer.

What we sell

One advertisement, declared. No tracking pixels, no fingerprinting, no email gates, no popups. We sell attention to advertisers in one clearly labelled box. That is the entire business model. Your inputs never leave your browser.
