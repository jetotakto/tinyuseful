/* MADE BY ONE PERSON */

  TinyUseful was built by a human, in a text editor,
  in 2026, the year everyone else was generating
  content with AI. The calculators do their math
  in your browser using formulas — not models.

  No tracking pixels. No popups. No email gates.
  No "intelligent assistant" that's actually a chatbot.
  No autoplay video ads. No newsletter prompts.

  We use Cloudflare's cookieless server-side analytics
  (page views, no personal identification, no cross-site
  follow). Read the full details in /privacy.html.

  Fonts (Newsreader, JetBrains Mono) are self-hosted in
  /fonts/. No Google Fonts CDN, no third-party requests.

  One advertisement, declared. That's the business.

  If you find a bug or have an idea: contact@tinyuseful.app.
